MOON NIGHT

Moon Night is a mobile app designed to address the issue of sleep deprivation, users can track sleep, track room temperature, experiment with meditation, relaxing music, and other methods.

This was created as part of a project organized by the Zuri team and ingressive4good. We were asked to pick a sector and solve a problem. I choose to solve a problem in the health sector because of my background as a health student.

Role

Solo student project for the I4GXZuri team internship training

Timeline

4 Weeks (June-July 2022)

Tools

  • Figma
  • Slack
  • Google suits

PROBLEM STATEMENT

College student fail to accomplish their personal goals.

As a college student, I noticed how many of my peers and I were not accomplishing our personal goals due to loss of sleep. This sparked the question: How to solve the problem of sleep loss?

SOLUTION

Sleep tracker

  • See how much sleep you are getting.
  • Helps you make informed decisions on your health.
  • It helps you understand yourself.

Meditation

  • Increases self awareness.
  • Reduces negative emotions.
  • Increasing patience and tolerance.

Environment tracker

  • Improve sleep quality.
  • Helps you combat insomnia episodes.
  • The metabolism slows down, and we spend less energy during sleep.

TESTING + IMPROVEMENTS

Based on the feedback I got from my peers and other instructors by doing usability testing, I made some improvements.

I included smartwatch support

Moon Night functions can be accessed easily from your wrist. Users can monitor their sleeping patterns using bluetooth pairing. This functionality can not be passed on easily because when worn at night, users can track sleep patterns. They can let users know how many hours of sleep they are getting and how many uninterrupted hours.
